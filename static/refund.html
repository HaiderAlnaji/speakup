Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: {{LAST_UPDATED}}

1. How Pro Access Works

SpeakUp Pro is sold in two possible ways, depending on your region and how you pay:

  • Local payment (ZainCash or Qi Card / "Pay with SuperQi") — a one-time payment that unlocks Pro access for 30 days. It does not renew automatically; when the 30 days end, you simply pay again if you want to continue. There is no subscription to "cancel" with this method.
  • Paddle (where available) — a recurring monthly subscription that renews automatically until you cancel.

2. Cancelling a Paddle Subscription

If you paid via Paddle, you can cancel anytime before your next renewal date to avoid being charged again. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep Pro access until the end of the period you already paid for.

3. Refund Eligibility

If you believe you were charged in error, were double-charged, or Pro features were not delivered after a successful payment, contact us within 3 days of the charge and we'll review it. Refunds are generally not available once you have substantially used Pro features during the paid period, except where required by law.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}} with the email address on your account and the approximate date of payment. For local payments (ZainCash / Qi Card), refunds are processed manually through the payment provider's own merchant reversal process, which can take a few business days. For Paddle, refunds are processed through Paddle as the merchant of record.

5. Failed or Unconfirmed Payments

SpeakUp only grants Pro access after independently confirming a successful payment with the provider. If a payment fails, is cancelled, or times out, you are not charged and Pro is not granted — nothing further is needed on your end.

6. Chargebacks

If you have an issue with a payment, please contact us first — we're happy to resolve billing problems directly. Filing a chargeback without contacting us first can delay a resolution.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as SpeakUp's payment options change. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.

8. Contact

Questions about a payment or refund? Email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}.
