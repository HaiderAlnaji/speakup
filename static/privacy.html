Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{LAST_UPDATED}}

1. What This Covers

This policy explains what data SpeakPort collects, why, and how it's protected. SpeakPort is a small, independently-run app — we collect as little as we need to run the Service.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: your email address and a securely hashed password (we never store your actual password — only a one-way hash).

Practice data: when you complete a practice attempt, we save the lesson/phrase you practiced, your similarity score, and a short excerpt of the transcript your browser produced, so we can show your progress over time.

Payment status only: if you upgrade to Pro, we store whether your payment succeeded and a transaction reference from the payment provider (ZainCash, Qi Card, or Paddle). We never see or store your card number, wallet PIN, or OTP codes — those are entered directly with the payment provider, never with SpeakPort.

3. Microphone and Speech Recognition

SpeakPort uses your browser's built-in speech recognition (a browser feature, not a SpeakPort server) to score your pronunciation, and your browser's built-in text-to-speech to play native audio. In Shadow Mode, your microphone recording is kept temporarily in your browser (as an in-memory audio clip) so you can compare it to the native recording — it is not uploaded to or stored on our servers.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

SpeakPort does not use tracking cookies, ads, or third-party analytics. We use your browser's local storage only to keep you signed in (a login token) and to remember simple preferences (like a language setting, once available). This data stays on your device.

5. How We Use Your Information

To run your account and let you sign in, to save and show your practice progress, to unlock Pro features after a verified payment, and to respond if you contact us for support.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertisers.

6. Payment Providers

When you pay for Pro, you're redirected to the payment provider's own page (ZainCash, Qi Card, or Paddle) to complete the payment. Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing the payment details you give them directly — SpeakPort never receives or stores that information, only a success/fail status.

7. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored in a database we control, protected with hashed passwords and signed login tokens. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable, industry-standard steps to protect your information.

8. Data Retention

We keep your account and practice data for as long as your account is active, so your progress is preserved. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

9. Your Rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your account data at any time by emailing {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}. We'll act on verified requests promptly.

10. Children's Privacy

SpeakPort is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them.

11. International Users

SpeakPort is operated from Iraq. If you use SpeakPort from another country, your information may be processed in Iraq or wherever our hosting provider's servers are located.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as SpeakPort changes. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}.
